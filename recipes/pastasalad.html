Awesome Pasta Salad

Homepage

Description

Pasta salad is a refreshing and versatile dish that combines cooked pasta with a mix of fresh vegetables, herbs, and a flavorful dressing. It’s a popular side dish for picnics, potlucks, and barbecues, and it can also be served as a light main course. The base typically includes short pasta like rotini or penne, mixed with ingredients such as cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, olives, and cheese. The variety of colors and textures makes pasta salad both visually appealing and delicious.

To prepare the salad, the pasta is first cooked and then cooled to room temperature. Once cooled, it is tossed with chopped vegetables and coated in a zesty dressing—usually made from olive oil, vinegar, mustard, and seasonings. Some variations include protein options like grilled chicken or chickpeas. Pasta salad is best served chilled, and it tastes even better after being refrigerated for a few hours to allow the flavors to blend. It’s an easy, make-ahead recipe that’s perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rotini pasta (or any short pasta)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, cubed (or mini mozzarella balls)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley or basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar (or lemon juice)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool it down quickly.

  2. Prepare the vegetables

    While the pasta cooks, wash and chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives. Cut the cheese into small cubes if needed.

  3. Make the dressing

    In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar (or lemon juice), mustard (if using), salt, and pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  4. Combine all ingredients

    In a large mixing bowl, add the cooled pasta, chopped vegetables, cheese, and fresh herbs. Pour the dressing over the salad.

  5. Toss the salad

    Gently toss everything together until the pasta is well coated and the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  6. Chill before serving

    Cover the bowl and refrigerate the salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.

  7. Serve and enjoy

    Give it a final toss before serving. Optionally garnish with extra herbs or a sprinkle of grated cheese.
